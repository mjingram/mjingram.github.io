Python War Game and the Probability of Winning

This simulates the War Card game. It also calculates the players probability of winning based on the cards you are initially dealt. The deck weight is calculated by (9,10,J,Q,K,A) increasing the weight by (1,2,3,4,5,6) respectively and (2,3,4,5,6) decreasing the weight by (-6,-5,-4,-3,-2,-1).The model used to calculate the probability is:

Prob of Winning = 7.67x10^(-3) x Deck Weight + 0.504

Theory behind this comes from this article: https://www.scq.ubc.ca/predictability-in-the-game-of-war/

Note: This only accounts for a regular and double war. In the rare case of a triple war, the computer automatically wins.
