About Me

I am a data analyst currently located in Denver, CO. I recently graduated from University of Colorado Denver with a Masters degree in statistcs. I also have a Bachelors degree in statistics from Colorado State University. Post graduation I worked as a data analyst at T Carta Marine, a geospatial company, in Denver.

I have extensive experience in data analysis and statistics, working with many different fields, including machine learning and biostatistics. For example, my master's thesis used machine learning and natural language processing to classify political articles to their news source. In addition, I did consulting work for the Denver Museum of Nature and Science where we published a research paper in statistical genetics focusing on how people with different genes experience taste.

In my personal time, I can be found hiking, cooking up a new recipe or reading up on science and technology. I am also a saxophone player and guitarist who loves to perform or attend live music. Feel free to contact me for work or questions. See below or the Contacts page for my information.
